# Gimlet

* **60 ml** gin ( *Tanqueray No. 10* )
* **15 ml** lime cordial ( *Rose's* )
* Lime twist

## Variations

Make your own cordial with Lime and Simple syrup. Final measures:

* **60 ml** Gin
* **15 ml** Lime juice
* **10 ml** Simple syrup
* Lime twist
