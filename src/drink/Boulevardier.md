# Boulevardier

* **30 ml** rye whiskey ( *Willet* ) or bourbon whiskey
* **30 ml** sweet red vermouth ( *Antica Formula* )
* **25 ml** *Campari*
* Orange twist

## Variations

* **30 ml** rye whiskey
* **30 ml** sweet red vermouth
* **25 ml** *Gran Classico*
* Orange twist

> The Gran Classico is less astringint and more orange flavored than Campari.
