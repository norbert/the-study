# Negroni

* **1 shot** gin ( *Tanqueray No. 10* )
* **1 shot** sweet red vermouth ( *Antica Formula* )
* **1 shot** *Campari*
* Orange twist

Pour all ingredients directly into large tumbler filled with ice. Stir gently.
Garnish with orange zest.
